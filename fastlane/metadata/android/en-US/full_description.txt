TravelLog automatically records your day as you explore.

AUTOMATIC GPS TRACKING
Records your route from 8 AM to midnight every day. Your track is saved as a GPX file so you can import it into any mapping app.

SMART MEDIA ASSOCIATION
Import photos and videos from your gallery. TravelLog links each one to a place using GPS EXIF data, track interpolation, or your last check-in — whichever is most accurate.

VOICE NOTES WITH LOCATION
Record voice notes on the go. Each note is pinned to your GPS position and associated with the nearest point of interest.

POINTS OF INTEREST
Nearby places are fetched automatically as you move. Check in to a café, museum, or park to anchor your photos and notes to that location.

OFFLINE MAP
View your full route, visited places, and voice note pins on a smooth vector map — no internet required for the map itself.

PDF EXPORT
Export any day as a travel blog: route summary, places visited, photos, and voice note transcriptions — ready to share or print.

GPX SHARING
Share your track and POI files directly to any app that reads GPX.

NO ADS. NO CLOUD. NO API KEYS.
Everything stays on your device. The map uses OpenStreetMap data via OpenFreeMap — completely free and private.
