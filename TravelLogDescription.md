The app should be capable of:

- Record the gps track continously. Starting 8:00 am to 0:00
- Store the gps track one per day
- Relate the photos and videos with the Travel log
- provide a function to record a voice note. Anotating the position, altitude, time where the voice note is recorded
- store automatically the Point of interest near the place where the voice note is recorded. Store this POI in a separate GPX, as points, not tracks. One file of POIS per day
- Provide a list of points of interest near the current position.
- provide a mechanism to do the check in the point of interest
- For every point of interest associate the photos videos and voice notes automatically.
- Show the track recorded of the current day on a map along the points of interest checked in
- Show an option to switch between current day or previous days to visualize them on the map.
- Show on the map: videos, pois checked, videos, voice notes
- Provide a button to export a blog of the day automatically to a pdf file
